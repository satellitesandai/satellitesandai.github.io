The Exoplanets Hub

Explore and learn about Exoplanets in 3D Space!

How to use this Website:

Explore

The interactive plot provides an approach to the understanding of exoplanet discovery by visualizing the spatial relation of discovered exoplanets. Each point represents an exoplanet according to its relative position in space. This immersive experience allows you to explore exoplanets and gain an understanding of how exploration missions shed light into the cosmos around us.

Features

View detailed information on each exoplanet by clicking on individual points, which will then appear in the details tab.

Refine the displayed exoplanet data by applying specific criteria in the filter tab.

Controls

Left-click and drag to rotate the 3D view. Scroll to zoom in and out. Hold Ctrl or right-click and drag to pan across the plot. Further control options can be found on the top right.

Reports

Select and read a report of your interest from the report hub. Scientific Reports are continuously added to this website.

Data courtesy of NASA's Exoplanet Archive. For more information, visit NASA Exoplanet Archive.
